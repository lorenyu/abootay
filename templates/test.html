About Me

I'm a product manager at Experience Project. I graduated in December 2009 from Stanford University with a masters in computer science with my concentration in human-computer interaction. I was born and grew up in L.A. county, and went to a Montessori school for kindergarten and elementary school. Afterwards, I went to high school at Flintridge Preparatory School in La Canada.

I love to draw, and I also really enjoy playing the guitar. Back in high school I liked to breakdance.

My favorite sport is basketball, but I also like table tennis. I also enjoy playing whatever: tennis, soccer, volleyball, ultimate frisbee, etc., but I'm not a fan of baseball. I like skiing too, but I suck at it and I don't really like cold weather.
